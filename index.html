A bit about me...

Hi! I'm Renuka from Goa, India. I love designing and drawing. I can be found either behind my computer or easel at any time of the day. Other than these I like cooking and reading. I have been a designer/front-end-developer for the past 4.6 years. I've had a lot of fun doing a lot of design and art over the years and you can find the best of them here, in my portfolio. The rest of them you can find in either Deviant Art or Behance.

I've worked as a faculty for Zee Institute of Creative Art, a designer + developer for VMA Technologies Pvt. Ltd., a front end developer for Origin Designext and Imager Technologies and currently I'm working as a UI designer + front end developer for Ajency.in.

Me and my husband run a Facebook Page where we together share our work. He's an awesome poet and a graphic designer ;)
